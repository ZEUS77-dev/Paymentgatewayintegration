What we do?

We are first to serve, wherever animals are in need of rescue, shelter, protection or security.Our mission is to end the human/street animal conflict. We provide several key programs to achieve our goals: spay/neuter, rabies vaccination, rescue and adoption, and community education for rabies safety and compassion. We depend on donations to continuously expand our work. Our aim is to reach out to every student, during their impressionable years, year after year, so that they eventually grow up to be compassionate citizens.
